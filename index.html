The Problem

Nature is worth crores — but shows up as zero

When a collector weighs building a highway through a wetland, only the road's value appears in the report. The wetland's flood protection, water filtration, fisheries, and carbon sequestration — worth ₹5.65 crore per 100 hectares every year — are invisible.

EcoValue India makes those numbers real, comparable, and policy-ready. Sourced from TEEB India, FSI ISFR 2023, TERI, and MoEFCC — the same data the government uses.
